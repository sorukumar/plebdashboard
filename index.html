Visualizing Lightning Network

PlebDashboard is a dashboard for the Lightning network. I aim to create intuitive views and visualizations and make lightning data more accessible.
Category The Questions that you can get answers to? Link
Node In one page, you get to see all the active nodes in LN. It has key node data like # of channels, total capacity, and capacity percentile. Almost everything you want to know in one page. Node Visualization
Node Lorenz curve of LN. We have plotted the Lorenz curve based on capacity and also Channels. You will learn that 1% of top nodes control 70% of the capacity of LN Lorenz Curve for Lightning Network
Node A bar chart on nodes grouped by capacity buckets. You can find out what % of nodes have more or less than X sats in capacity, and also what the distribution looks like. Distribution of Nodes by Capacity
Node A bar chart on nodes grouped by channel count buckets. You can find out what % of nodes have more or less than X number of channels Distribution of Nodes by Channel

Channels In one page, you get to see all the channels in LN. Hover over it to find out the nodes connected by the channel, the size of the node, and the fee. Channels visualized in one page
Channels This has data on the distribution of # of channels by fee rate bucket. You can also find out, let's say, what % of channels have less than 10ppm fee rate. Distribution of Channels by fee rate
Channels This has data on the distribution of # of channels by fee rate bucket. You can also find out, let's say, that size of 80% of channels is less than 10M sats. Distribution of Channels by capacity buckets
